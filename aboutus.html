About Us

WHAT WE OFFER?

CafeBeans offers a unique coffee house environment. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee. We are a place where you can sit and enjoy a tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specially for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area infront of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body. We offer a delicious variety of coffee made by our professionally trained baristas. All of our sauces and syrups are made-in house with all natural ingredients ensuring you the highest quality in taste and health. We are looking forward for serving you.

WHO ARE OUR PEOPLE?

Our people are the kindest, most hardworking and talented in the business. We are an impossibly eclectic group of coffee experts, artists, writers, sensory scientists, bakers, designers, and all-around dreamers.
